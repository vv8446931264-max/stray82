About Stray8

Stray8 is more than just a brand; it's a canvas for self-expression. Born from a passion for sneaker culture, anime, and vibrant graffiti art, we specialize in transforming ordinary sneakers into extraordinary, wearable masterpieces. Every pair is meticulously hand-painted or printed locally in India, ensuring premium quality and a unique touch. We believe your sneakers should tell your story, be it a beloved anime character, a bold graffiti tag, or a minimalist statement. At Stray8, no two sneakers are the same – because your individuality deserves a one-of-a-kind design. Join us in wearing what you want.
